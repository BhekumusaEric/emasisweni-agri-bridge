Our Programs

Comprehensive agricultural and sustainability solutions

Agriculture Unit

Empowering farmers with skills, resources, and market access

Training & Mentorship

Comprehensive agricultural training programs with ongoing mentorship support for sustainable farming practices.

Food Security Initiatives

School and community food gardens that provide nutrition while teaching sustainable agriculture.

Agro-Processing

Value-addition opportunities that help farmers process and package their produce for better market prices.

Market Access

Connecting smallholder farmers directly to commercial markets and buyers for fair pricing.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Specialized programs targeting youth and women in agriculture for inclusive development.

Climate-Smart Farming

Research and promotion of climate-resilient farming practices adapted to local conditions.

Sustainability & Waste Management Unit

Creating circular economy solutions for environmental protection

Agricultural Waste Management

Collection, segregation and recycling of crop residues, farm plastics, livestock waste and agro-processing by-products.

Community Waste Management

Establishment of local collection points, recycling initiatives and circular economy models that generate income for rural households.

Organic Composting

Development of composting hubs that convert organic waste into nutrient-rich compost to support soil regeneration.

Waste-to-Energy Solutions

Exploration of biogas digesters and bioenergy technologies for livestock farms and community food waste systems.

Environmental Education

Training, workshops and school programmes focusing on sustainability, climate resilience, and waste management.

Environmental Compliance

Support for rural communities and agricultural enterprises to comply with environmental legislation.
